# Q2 2025 Financial Performance Report

## Executive Summary

AcmeCorp achieved consolidated revenue of 127.5 billion VND in Q2 2025, representing a 14.2% year-over-year increase. Operating margin improved to 22.8%, up from 19.1% in Q2 2024, driven by cost optimization in cloud infrastructure and headcount efficiency gains.

Key highlights:
- Revenue growth accelerated from 11.8% (Q1) to 14.2% (Q2)
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC) decreased by 18% quarter-over-quarter
- Net revenue retention rate reached 118%, up from 112% in Q1
- Free cash flow of 28.3 billion VND (22.2% FCF margin)

### Cost of Revenue Analysis

Cloud infrastructure costs decreased as a percentage of revenue (from 31.7% to 30.0%) despite absolute growth, driven by:
- Migration of 35% of compute workloads to reserved instances (saving 2.1B VND/quarter)
- Improved container orchestration reducing idle compute by 22%
- Renegotiated bandwidth contracts with tier-1 providers

### R&D Investment

R&D spending increased to 23.0% of revenue, reflecting investment in:
- Generative AI platform (12 new engineers hired in Q2)
- Real-time data streaming infrastructure
- Edge computing capabilities for Southeast Asian markets
- Security and compliance automation tools
