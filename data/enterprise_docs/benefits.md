### 1. Purpose

This guide provides a comprehensive overview of employee benefits at GravityTech Vietnam. Our benefits package is designed to support employee well-being, professional growth, and work-life balance, reflecting our commitment as a leading Australian-invested technology company operating in Vietnam.

All benefits are effective from the employee's start date unless otherwise noted. Part-time employees (working 20+ hours/week) receive pro-rated benefits.

---

### 2. Health & Medical Benefits

#### 2.1 Health Insurance

- **Provider:** PVI Insurance Corporation - **Premium Healthcare Plan**
- **Coverage:** Employee + up to **2 dependents** (spouse and/or children under 18, or under 23 if enrolled in full-time education).
- **Annual coverage limit:** 500,000,000 VND per insured person.
- **Inpatient coverage:** 100% of hospital room and board (semi-private room), surgery, and ICU charges up to the annual limit.
- **Outpatient coverage:** 80% reimbursement for doctor visits, prescriptions, and diagnostic tests at network hospitals. Maximum 30 outpatient visits per year.
- **Network hospitals include:** FV Hospital, Vinmec, University Medical Center, Franco-Vietnamese Hospital (HCMC); Vinmec Times City, French Hospital (Hanoi).
- **Premium cost:** 100% company-paid for the employee. Dependent coverage is 70% company-paid; the remaining 30% is deducted from the employee's salary (approximately 350,000 VND/month per dependent).
- Enrollment: Employees must enroll dependents within **30 days of their start date** or within 30 days of a qualifying life event (marriage, birth of child).

#### 2.2 Dental Coverage

- Dental care is covered under a **separate dental rider** on the PVI plan.
- **Annual cap:** 5,000,000 VND per insured person.
- Coverage includes: routine check-ups (2 per year), fillings, extractions, and basic restorative work.
- **Excluded:** Orthodontics, cosmetic dentistry, and implants.
- Dental claims are submitted via the PVI mobile app or email within 30 days of treatment.

#### 2.3 Annual Health Check

- All employees receive a **comprehensive annual health check** at **Vinmec International Hospital**.
- The health check is scheduled annually in **October-November** and includes:
  - General physical examination
  - Blood panel (CBC, lipid profile, liver/kidney function, blood glucose)
  - Chest X-ray
  - Abdominal ultrasound
  - ECG (for employees 35+)
  - Eye and hearing tests
  - Cancer screening markers (for employees 40+)
- **Estimated value:** 3,500,000 - 5,000,000 VND per employee depending on age-based add-ons.
- Employees may bring dependents for a health check at the **company-negotiated rate** (approximately 40% discount off standard pricing).

#### 2.4 Mental Health Support

- Access to **Calm** premium app subscription (company-paid).
- **4 sessions per year** with a licensed counselor through our Employee Assistance Program (EAP), provided by International SOS.
- EAP services are **confidential** and available 24/7 via hotline: **1800-599-100**.

### 3. Fitness & Wellness

#### 3.1 Gym Allowance

- All employees receive a monthly **gym/fitness allowance** of **500,000 VND per month** (6,000,000 VND annually).
- The allowance can be used for: gym memberships, yoga/pilates classes, swimming, martial arts, personal training, or sports league fees.
- Reimbursed monthly upon submission of a valid receipt via the expense system (SAP Concur).
- The allowance is **non-cumulative**: unused monthly amounts do not carry over.

#### 3.2 Wellness Initiatives

- **Weekly fruit delivery** to all office locations (Monday and Wednesday).
- **Quarterly wellness challenges** with prizes (step challenges, meditation streaks, etc.).
- **Standing desk** available on request for all employees at no cost.
- **Ergonomic assessment** provided by an occupational therapist within 30 days of joining.

### 4. Professional Development

#### 4.1 Learning & Development Budget

- Every employee receives an annual **learning budget of 10,000,000 VND** (approximately 400 USD).
- The budget covers: online courses (Udemy, Coursera, LinkedIn Learning), conference tickets, books, certification exams, and workshop fees.
- Unused learning budget does **not carry over** to the next year.
- Requests exceeding the annual budget may be approved by the department head on a case-by-case basis.
- The company also provides a **team-level training budget** managed by department heads for group workshops and external trainers.

#### 4.2 Certification Support

- For **approved professional certifications** (e.g., AWS Solutions Architect, PMP, CISSP, CFA), the company covers:
  - 100% of the exam fee (first attempt).
  - 50% of the exam fee (second attempt, if needed).
  - Study materials up to 3,000,000 VND.
- Employees who earn an approved certification receive a **one-time bonus of 2,000,000 VND**.

#### 4.3 Conference Attendance

- Each team is allocated budget for **1-2 employees per year** to attend a relevant industry conference (domestic or international).
- For international conferences, the company covers: registration, economy airfare, hotel (up to 3,000,000 VND/night for 3 nights), and a per diem of 1,500,000 VND/day.

### 5. Work Flexibility

#### 5.1 Remote Work Policy

- Employees at **L2 (Mid-Level) and above** are eligible to work remotely up to **2 days per week**.
- L1 (Junior) employees may work remotely **1 day per week** after completing their 90-day probation.
- Remote work days must be agreed upon with the direct manager and registered in BambooHR by the preceding Friday.
- **Core collaboration hours:** All employees (remote or in-office) must be available on Slack and reachable between **10:00 AM and 4:00 PM (ICT / UTC+7)**, Monday to Friday.
- Fully remote arrangements (5 days/week) are available for exceptional cases with VP-level approval and are reviewed quarterly.

#### 5.2 Flexible Hours

- Standard working hours are **9:00 AM to 6:00 PM** (1 hour lunch break).
- Employees may adjust their start time within a **flex window of 8:00 AM to 10:00 AM**, provided they complete 8 working hours and are available during core hours.

### 6. Company Events & Team Building

#### 6.1 Company Trips

GravityTech organizes **2 company-wide trips per year**:

| Trip | Timing | Typical Destination | Budget per Person |
|------|--------|-------------------|-------------------|
| **Summer Trip** | June-July | Beach resort (Da Nang, Phu Quoc, Nha Trang) | 5,000,000 VND |
| **Year-End Party & Trip** | December | Mountain/highland (Da Lat, Sa Pa) or resort | 7,000,000 VND |

- Trips include transportation, accommodation (2-3 nights), meals, and team activities.
- Attendance is voluntary but strongly encouraged. Employees who choose not to attend do not receive a monetary equivalent.

#### 6.2 Team Building Budget

- Each team receives a **quarterly team-building budget** of **300,000 VND per person**.
- Activities may include: team dinners, bowling, escape rooms, cooking classes, karaoke, or outdoor activities.
- Budget is managed by the team lead and expensed via SAP Concur.

#### 6.3 Holiday Celebrations

- **Tet (Lunar New Year):** Company-wide celebration with lucky money envelopes (li xi) for all employees, typically 200,000 - 500,000 VND.
- **International Women's Day (March 8):** Gifts and activities for female employees.
- **Mid-Autumn Festival:** Mooncake gift boxes for all employees and their families.
- **Christmas / Year-End:** Combined with the year-end trip and party.

### 8. Benefits Enrollment & Questions

- Benefits enrollment is managed through **BambooHR**. New employees must complete enrollment within **14 days** of their start date.
- Insurance cards (PVI) are issued within **10 business days** of enrollment.
- For benefits-related questions, contact the HR Operations team at **benefits@gravitytech.com.vn** or Slack channel **#ask-benefits**.
- Benefits information sessions are held quarterly (January, April, July, October) for new joiners and interested employees.
