## Annual Budget Plan - Fiscal Year 2026

**Prepared by:** Finance Department, Gravity Engineering Solutions JSC  
**Approval Date:** December 10, 2025  
**Approved by:** Board of Directors  
**Budget Period:** January 1 - December 31, 2026  
**Document ID:** FIN-BUD-2026-001

---

### Executive Summary

The Board of Directors has approved a total budget of **180 billion VND** for Fiscal Year 2026, representing a 22% increase over FY2025 actual expenditures of 147.5 billion VND. This budget supports the company's strategic growth objectives, including geographic expansion into the Australian and Southeast Asian markets, acceleration of R&D investment in AI-driven engineering tools, and a significant headcount increase of **45 new hires** across all departments.

Key capital investments include a new 1,200 sqm office facility in District 7, Ho Chi Minh City and substantial upgrades to cloud infrastructure to support remote delivery capabilities.

#### Engineering (85B - 47%)

The Engineering department receives the largest allocation at 85 billion VND, covering salaries for 185 engineers (current 160 + 25 new hires), project delivery costs, subcontractor fees, and specialized software licenses. The budget includes 4.5 billion VND for professional development and technical certifications (AWS, Azure, PMP, PE licenses). A dedicated 2 billion VND allocation supports the new Mining Automation Practice being established in Q1 2026.

#### Sales & Marketing (35B - 19%)

Sales & Marketing receives a 30% YoY increase to 35 billion VND, reflecting the company's aggressive growth strategy. This includes 12 billion VND for the new Australia/NZ regional sales team (3 sales directors + support staff), 8 billion VND for digital marketing and brand awareness campaigns, 6 billion VND for trade show participation (OzMining Expo, CEBIT Australia, Vietnam Tech Summit), and 9 billion VND for existing sales operations in Vietnam and Singapore.

#### Operations (25B - 14%)

The Operations budget of 25 billion VND covers office leases (Hanoi HQ: 4.2B, HCMC office: 3.8B, Singapore office: 5.1B), utilities, facilities management, IT infrastructure maintenance, and general operational support. The new District 7 office build-out is budgeted separately under Capital Expenditure.

#### Research & Development (20B - 11%)

R&D investment increases 45% to 20 billion VND, the largest percentage increase of any department. Priority projects include: the AI-Assisted Structural Analysis Platform (8B), IoT sensor integration framework for mining applications (5B), automated compliance checking engine for Vietnamese building codes (4B), and exploratory research in digital twin technology (3B). The R&D team is expected to grow from 22 to 32 engineers.

#### Administration (15B - 8%)

Administration covers HR, Legal, Finance, and executive office operations. Key line items include: payroll processing and HR systems (3.2B), legal and compliance (2.8B), insurance premiums (2.1B), external audit fees (1.5B), and executive operations (5.4B).

### Contingency Reserve (5B)

A **contingency reserve of 5 billion VND** (2.8% of total budget) is maintained for unforeseen expenses, including:
- Currency fluctuation impacts on international contracts (estimated exposure: 35% of revenue in AUD/USD)
- Potential project cost overruns beyond 15% of budgeted amounts
- Emergency infrastructure repairs or replacements
- Market-driven salary adjustments to retain key talent

Drawdown from the contingency reserve requires CFO approval for amounts up to 2 billion VND and Board approval for amounts exceeding 2 billion VND.
