| 7.2 | Annual **vendor security reassessment** for critical vendors | Annually | IT Security + Procurement | ☐ |

| 7.5 | Review **vendor access rights** and deprovisioning | Quarterly | IT Security | ☐ |
| 7.6 | Conduct **vendor business continuity** assessment | Annually | Procurement + SRE | ☐ |
| 7.7 | Review **sub-processor** list for data processing vendors | Quarterly | DPO + Procurement | ☐ |

### Vendor Risk Tiers

| Tier | Criteria | Assessment Requirements |
|---|---|---|
| **Critical** | Access to customer data, production systems, or revenue-impacting | Full security assessment, annual audit, SLA monitoring |
| **High** | Access to internal systems or employee data | Security questionnaire, annual review |
| **Medium** | Limited system access, no sensitive data | Self-assessment questionnaire |
| **Low** | No system access, no data processing | Standard contract terms only |

### Approved Assessment Frameworks

- **SIG (Standardized Information Gathering)** questionnaire for new vendors.
- **CAIQ (Consensus Assessments Initiative Questionnaire)** for cloud vendors.
- **Custom security assessment** for Critical-tier vendors.

## 8. Quarterly Review Process

### Timeline

| Week | Activity |
|---|---|
| Week 1 | Department heads complete their sections of the checklist |
| Week 2 | Compliance team consolidates responses and identifies gaps |
| Week 3 | Gap remediation plans developed with responsible parties |
| Week 4 | **Compliance Review Board** meeting (Compliance Officer, CISO, CFO, Legal Counsel) |

### Documentation

- Completed checklists are stored in **Confluence > Legal > Compliance > Quarterly Reviews**.
- Gap remediation is tracked in **Jira** under the `COMPLIANCE` project.
- Board meeting minutes retained for **5 years**.

### Escalation

| Gap Severity | Escalation Path | Remediation Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| Critical | CEO + Board immediately | **7 days** |
| High | VP-level + Compliance Officer | **30 days** |
| Medium | Department Head + Compliance | **60 days** |
| Low | Compliance tracking | **90 days** |
