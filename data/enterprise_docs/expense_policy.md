## Corporate Expense Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Last Revised:** March 15, 2025  
**Policy Owner:** Finance Department, Gravity Engineering Solutions JSC  
**Applies To:** All employees, contractors, and consultants  
**Document ID:** FIN-POL-2025-003

---

### 1. Purpose and Scope

This policy establishes the guidelines and approval requirements for all business-related expenses incurred by employees of Gravity Engineering Solutions JSC. It applies to all domestic and international travel, client entertainment, procurement of goods and services, and any other expenditure made on behalf of the company.

All employees are expected to exercise sound judgment and fiscal responsibility when incurring business expenses. Expenses must be reasonable, necessary, and directly related to legitimate business purposes.

---

### 2. Approval Tiers

All expenses must be pre-approved according to the following thresholds:

| Expense Amount (VND)       | Approver                        | Response SLA   |
|----------------------------|---------------------------------|----------------|
| Less than 5,000,000 (<5M)  | Team Lead                       | 1 business day |
| 5,000,000 - 20,000,000 (5-20M)  | Department Head            | 2 business days|
| 20,000,000 - 50,000,000 (20-50M) | Chief Financial Officer (CFO) | 3 business days|
| Over 50,000,000 (>50M)     | CEO + Board Approval            | 5 business days|

For recurring expenses (e.g., monthly subscriptions, ongoing vendor contracts), approval is required once annually at the aggregate annual value. Any single expense that exceeds the pre-approved amount by more than 10% requires re-approval at the appropriate tier.

Emergency expenses up to 10,000,000 VND may be incurred without prior approval but must be submitted with justification within 48 hours for retroactive approval by the Department Head.

---

### 3. Travel Policy

#### 3.1 Air Travel

- **Domestic flights:** Economy class is required for all domestic travel within Vietnam, regardless of employee level.
- **International flights (6 hours or less):** Economy class for all employees. Premium economy may be approved by the CFO for medical accommodations.
- **International flights (more than 6 hours):** Business class is permitted for flights exceeding 6 hours in duration. This applies to both direct flights and the longest segment of connecting itineraries.
- **Booking:** All flights must be booked through the designated corporate travel agent (SaigonTravel Corporate) or via the company's Traveloka Business account at least 7 days in advance. Last-minute bookings (less than 72 hours) require Department Head approval regardless of cost.

#### 3.2 Accommodation

- **Domestic:** Maximum of 2,500,000 VND per night in Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City; 1,800,000 VND per night in other provinces.
- **International:** Actual cost up to the city-specific cap published quarterly by Finance. Current caps range from USD 150-250/night depending on destination.
- **Extended stay (>5 nights):** Serviced apartments or corporate housing arrangements should be explored for cost savings. Contact admin@company.com for approved options.

#### 3.3 Per Diem Allowance

Per diem covers meals, local transportation, and incidental expenses during business travel:

| Travel Type    | Per Diem Rate (VND/day) | Notes                                    |
|----------------|-------------------------|------------------------------------------|
| Domestic       | 1,500,000 (1.5M)       | Applies to all overnight domestic travel |
| International  | 3,000,000 (3M)         | Equivalent to approx. USD 120/day        |

Per diem is paid in advance for approved trips and covers breakfast, lunch, dinner, and local transit (taxis, ride-hailing). Per diem is not paid for days where meals are provided by the client, conference organizer, or company event. Half-day travel (departure after 12:00 or return before 12:00) qualifies for 50% of the daily rate.

#### 3.4 Ground Transportation

- Domestic: Grab/Be for rides under 500,000 VND; company vehicle or rental for longer distances.
- International: Public transportation or ride-hailing preferred. Rental cars require CFO pre-approval and must include comprehensive insurance.

---

### 4. Client Entertainment

- **Pre-approval required** for all client entertainment expenses, submitted via the Entertainment Request Form (FIN-F-007) at least 3 business days in advance.
- **Maximum spend:** 10,000,000 VND (10M) per event. Events exceeding this cap require CFO approval.
- The request must include: client name and title, business purpose, expected number of attendees, venue, and estimated cost.
- Alcohol expenses are permitted but must not exceed 30% of the total entertainment bill.
- Entertainment of government officials is subject to additional compliance review by the Legal department and must comply with anti-corruption regulations under Vietnamese law (Decree 59/2019).

---

### 5. Receipt and Documentation Requirements

- **Receipts are required for all expenses exceeding 500,000 VND (500K).** Original receipts or clear digital scans/photos are accepted.
- Expenses below 500,000 VND require only an itemized description in the expense report.
- All receipts must include: vendor name, date, itemized charges, total amount, and payment method.
- Credit card statements alone do not constitute sufficient documentation; itemized receipts are required.
- Missing receipts for expenses over 500,000 VND must be accompanied by a Missing Receipt Affidavit (FIN-F-012) signed by the employee's Department Head.

---

### 6. Reimbursement Process

- Expense reports must be submitted within **10 business days** of the expense date or return from travel.
- Approved reimbursements are processed within **14 business days** of submission via direct bank transfer to the employee's registered payroll account.
- Late submissions (beyond 30 days) may be rejected at the discretion of the Finance Department.
- Expenses incurred in foreign currencies are reimbursed at the Vietcombank exchange rate on the date of the transaction.

---

### 7. Corporate Credit Cards

- **Eligibility:** Corporate credit cards are issued to employees at **Level 3 (L3) and above** in the company's job grade structure.
- Monthly credit limits are set based on role and typical expense patterns:
  - L3: 20,000,000 VND/month
  - L4: 50,000,000 VND/month
  - L5 and above: 100,000,000 VND/month
- Corporate card statements must be reconciled and submitted monthly by the 5th business day of the following month.
- Personal use of the corporate credit card is strictly prohibited and may result in disciplinary action.
- Lost or stolen cards must be reported to Finance within 4 hours via the emergency hotline: 1900-xxxx.

---

### 8. Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following expenses are not reimbursable under any circumstances:
- Personal entertainment, gifts, or services
- Traffic fines or parking violations
- Airline lounge memberships (unless bundled with approved corporate card)
- Upgrades to premium cabins without prior approval
- Expenses for family members or non-employees
- Late fees or interest charges resulting from delayed personal payment

---

### 9. Policy Violations

Violations of this policy, including fraudulent expense claims, may result in:
- Denial of reimbursement
- Repayment of previously reimbursed amounts
- Disciplinary action up to and including termination
- Referral to legal authorities in cases of fraud

Employees who identify potential policy violations should report them to the Finance Department or the anonymous ethics hotline.
