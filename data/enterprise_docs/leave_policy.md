## Leave Policy - GravityTech Vietnam JSC

**Document ID:** HR-POL-001  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Last Revised:** March 15, 2025  
**Approved By:** Nguyen Thi Mai, Chief People Officer  

---

### 1. Purpose

This policy outlines the leave entitlements for all full-time employees of GravityTech Vietnam JSC, a subsidiary of GravityTech Australia Pty Ltd. It applies to all offices including Ho Chi Minh City (HQ), Hanoi, and Da Nang.

---

### 2. Annual Leave

All full-time employees are entitled to paid annual leave based on their years of continuous service:

| Tenure | Annual Leave Entitlement |
|--------|--------------------------|
| 0 - 3 years | 12 working days per year |
| 3 - 5 years | 15 working days per year |
| 5+ years | 18 working days per year |

- Annual leave accrues monthly (1 day per month for new employees, 1.25 days for 3-5 year tenure, 1.5 days for 5+ years).
- Employees may not take annual leave during the first 60 days of employment except in exceptional circumstances approved by their direct manager and HR.
- Leave requests of 3 or more consecutive days must be submitted at least 14 calendar days in advance via the HR Portal (BambooHR).
- Leave requests of 1-2 days require at least 3 business days' notice.

#### 2.1 Carry-Over Policy

- Employees may carry over a maximum of **5 unused annual leave days** to the following calendar year.
- Carried-over leave must be used by **March 31** of the following year. Any unused carried-over days will expire automatically and cannot be compensated.
- Leave days beyond the 5-day carry-over limit will be forfeited on December 31 of the current year.
- In exceptional cases, the Head of Department may approve extended carry-over up to 8 days with written justification submitted to HR by December 15.

---

### 3. Sick Leave

- All employees are entitled to **30 paid sick leave days per year**, in accordance with Vietnamese Labor Law.
- Sick leave of **3 or more consecutive days** requires a medical certificate from a licensed healthcare provider.
- Sick leave pay is calculated at **75% of the employee's base salary** for the first 15 days, and **65%** for days 16-30, funded through social insurance contributions.
- Employees must notify their direct manager and HR within **2 hours** of their scheduled start time on the first day of absence.
- Extended sick leave beyond 30 days may be covered under long-term disability provisions; employees should contact HR for guidance.

---

### 4. Maternity and Paternity Leave

#### 4.1 Maternity Leave
- Female employees are entitled to **6 months (180 calendar days)** of maternity leave, in compliance with Vietnamese Labor Law.
- Maternity leave may begin up to 2 months before the expected due date.
- Pay during maternity leave is covered by social insurance at 100% of the average salary of the preceding 6 months.
- Employees returning from maternity leave are guaranteed the same or equivalent position.
- An additional 30 days of unpaid maternity leave may be requested with manager approval.

#### 4.2 Paternity Leave
- Male employees are entitled to **5 paid working days** of paternity leave, to be taken within 30 days of the child's birth.
- For complicated births (cesarean section, premature birth, or multiple births), paternity leave is extended to **7 paid working days**.

---

### 5. Public Holidays

GravityTech Vietnam observes **11 public holidays** per year as mandated by Vietnamese law:

| Holiday | Date(s) | Days |
|---------|---------|------|
| New Year's Day | January 1 | 1 day |
| Tet (Lunar New Year) | 5 days around Lunar New Year (varies annually) | 5 days |
| Hung Kings' Commemoration | 10th day of 3rd lunar month | 1 day |
| Reunification Day | April 30 | 1 day |
| International Labour Day | May 1 | 1 day |
| National Day | September 2-3 | 2 days |

- If a public holiday falls on a weekend, employees receive the following Monday as a compensatory day off.
- Foreign employees receive an additional **1 day** for their national day, subject to documentation.

### 6. Other Leave Types

| Leave Type | Entitlement | Documentation Required |
|------------|-------------|----------------------|
| Bereavement (immediate family) | 3 paid days | Death certificate |
| Bereavement (extended family) | 1 paid day | Death certificate |
| Marriage (own) | 3 paid days | Marriage certificate |
| Marriage (child) | 1 paid day | Invitation/certificate |
| Jury duty / Court summons | As required | Court notice |
| Unpaid personal leave | Up to 10 days/year | Manager + HR approval |

---

### 7. Leave Request Process

1. Submit leave request through BambooHR at least the required notice period in advance.
2. Direct manager approves or declines within 2 business days.
3. HR records the approved leave and updates the team calendar.
4. For emergency leave, notify your manager by phone or chat (Slack/Teams) immediately, then submit a retroactive request within 24 hours of returning.
