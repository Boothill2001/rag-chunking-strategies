## Article 1: Definitions

**1.1 "Confidential Information"** means any and all non-public information disclosed by the Disclosing Party to the Receiving Party, whether orally, in writing, electronically, or by any other means, including but not limited to:

- **(a) Source Code:** All software source code, algorithms, architectures, system designs, database schemas, API specifications, and related technical documentation.
- **(b) Business Strategies:** Business plans, product roadmaps, market analysis, competitive intelligence, pricing strategies, and go-to-market plans.
- **(c) Client Data:** Customer lists, customer contracts, usage data, transaction records, and any personally identifiable information of customers or end users.
- **(d) Financial Information:** Revenue figures, profit margins, cost structures, investment plans, fundraising activities, valuation documents, and financial projections.
- **(e) Trade Secrets:** Proprietary methodologies, machine learning models, training datasets, unique processes, and any information that derives economic value from not being generally known.
- **(f) Employee Information:** Compensation data, organizational charts, talent acquisition strategies, and employee performance records.

**1.2 "Purpose"** means the evaluation and/or execution of a potential or existing business relationship between the Parties, as described in Schedule A attached hereto.

## Article 2: Duration

**2.1** This Agreement shall remain in effect for a period of **two (2) years** from the Effective Date ("Term"), unless earlier terminated in accordance with Article 8.

**2.2** The obligations of confidentiality shall survive the termination or expiration of this Agreement for an additional period of **one (1) year** (total protection period: 3 years from signing).

## Article 3: Obligations of the Receiving Party

**3.1** The Receiving Party shall:

- (a) Use the Confidential Information solely for the Purpose.
- (b) Protect the Confidential Information with at least the same degree of care as it protects its own confidential information, but no less than **reasonable care**.
- (c) Limit access to Confidential Information to employees and contractors who have a **need-to-know** and who are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as restrictive as this Agreement.
- (d) Not disclose, publish, or disseminate Confidential Information to any third party without the prior **written consent** of the Disclosing Party.
- (e) Not reverse-engineer, decompile, or disassemble any software, prototypes, or samples provided as Confidential Information.
- (f) Maintain a log of all persons who have accessed Confidential Information, available for audit upon request.

**3.2** The Receiving Party shall notify the Disclosing Party in writing within **48 hours** of becoming aware of any unauthorized disclosure or use of Confidential Information.

## Article 4: Exclusions from Confidential Information

The obligations under this Agreement do not apply to information that the Receiving Party can demonstrate:

- **(a)** Was publicly available at the time of disclosure, or subsequently became publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party.
- **(b)** Was independently developed by the Receiving Party without use of or reference to the Confidential Information, as evidenced by contemporaneous written records.
- **(c)** Was lawfully received from a third party without restriction on disclosure and without breach of any obligation of confidentiality.
- **(d)** Was already in the Receiving Party's possession prior to disclosure, as evidenced by written records predating the Effective Date.
- **(e)** Is required to be disclosed by law, regulation, or court order, provided that the Receiving Party gives the Disclosing Party **prompt written notice** (within 5 business days) and cooperates with efforts to obtain a protective order.

## Article 5: Intellectual Property

**5.1** No license, right, or interest in any intellectual property of the Disclosing Party is granted under this Agreement, expressly or by implication.

**5.2** All Confidential Information remains the exclusive property of the Disclosing Party.

**5.3** Any derivative works, analyses, or compilations created from the Confidential Information shall be deemed Confidential Information of the Disclosing Party.

## Article 6: Breach and Remedies

**6.1 Liquidated Damages:** In the event of a breach of this Agreement by the Receiving Party, the Receiving Party shall pay the Disclosing Party liquidated damages in the amount of **Five Hundred Million Vietnamese Dong (500,000,000 VND)**, in addition to compensation for any actual damages proven by the Disclosing Party.

**6.2 Injunctive Relief:** The Receiving Party acknowledges that a breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm for which monetary damages are inadequate, and the Disclosing Party shall be entitled to seek injunctive relief in addition to any other remedies.

**6.3 Indemnification:** The Receiving Party shall indemnify and hold harmless the Disclosing Party from all losses, damages, costs, and expenses (including reasonable legal fees) arising from or related to a breach of this Agreement.

## Article 7: Return of Materials

**7.1** Upon termination or expiration of this Agreement, or upon written request by the Disclosing Party at any time, the Receiving Party shall within **fourteen (14) days**:

- (a) Return all originals and copies of Confidential Information in tangible form.
- (b) Permanently delete all electronic copies of Confidential Information from all systems, devices, and backup media.
- (c) Provide a signed **Certificate of Destruction** confirming compliance with this Article.

**7.2** Notwithstanding the above, the Receiving Party may retain one (1) archival copy solely for legal compliance purposes, subject to continued confidentiality obligations.

## Article 8: Termination

**8.1** Either Party may terminate this Agreement by providing **thirty (30) days** prior written notice to the other Party.

**8.2** The Disclosing Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the Receiving Party breaches any material obligation under this Agreement.

**8.3** Termination does not affect any rights or obligations accrued prior to termination.

## Article 9: Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

**9.1 Governing Law:** This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the **laws of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam**.

**9.2 Dispute Resolution:** Any dispute arising out of or in connection with this Agreement shall be finally settled by arbitration administered by the **Vietnam International Arbitration Centre (VIAC)** at the Vietnam Chamber of Commerce and Industry, in accordance with VIAC's arbitration rules.

**9.3** The arbitration shall be conducted in **Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam**.

**9.4** The language of arbitration shall be **Vietnamese**, with English translation available upon request.

**9.5** The arbitral award shall be final and binding upon both Parties.

## Article 10: General Provisions

**10.1 Entire Agreement:** This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties regarding the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior agreements, understandings, and negotiations.

**10.2 Amendments:** No amendment to this Agreement shall be effective unless made in writing and signed by authorized representatives of both Parties.

**10.3 Assignment:** Neither Party may assign this Agreement without the prior written consent of the other Party.

**10.4 Severability:** If any provision of this Agreement is held invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full force and effect.

**10.5 Notices:** All notices shall be in writing and delivered by registered mail or email to the addresses specified above.
