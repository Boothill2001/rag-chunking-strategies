### 1. Overview

This guide outlines the structured onboarding program for all new employees joining GravityTech Vietnam. Our 30-60-90 day onboarding framework ensures new hires are integrated effectively into their teams and set up for success from day one.

All onboarding activities are tracked in the **Onboarding Checklist** on BambooHR. Managers and onboarding buddies share responsibility for ensuring each milestone is completed on schedule.

---

### 2. Pre-Boarding (Before Day 1)

The following must be completed by the HR and IT teams **at least 3 business days** before the new hire's start date:

- **Employment contract** signed and filed in BambooHR.
- **IT account provisioning**: email (Google Workspace), Slack, Jira, Confluence, GitHub/GitLab, VPN credentials.
- **Equipment order**: laptop and peripherals dispatched to office or home address.
- **Buddy assignment**: a peer-level colleague from the same or adjacent team, assigned by the hiring manager.
- **Welcome kit** prepared: company handbook, branded merchandise, office map, access badge request form.
- **Calendar invitations** sent for all Week 1 onboarding sessions.

### 5. The 30-60-90 Day Framework

#### 5.1 Day 30 Milestone: Department Integration Complete

**Objective:** The new hire understands the team's mission, ongoing projects, key stakeholders, and standard operating procedures.

**Expected outcomes by Day 30:**
- Completed all 3 mandatory training modules (Information Security, Anti-Harassment, Company Culture) with passing scores (minimum 80%).
- Met all direct team members and key cross-functional stakeholders (minimum 8 introductory meetings).
- Reviewed and understood at least 3 active project briefs relevant to their role.
- Contributed to at least 1 team meeting or sprint review.
- Buddy check-in completed (buddy submits feedback form to HR).
- Manager conducts a **30-day check-in** meeting to review progress and address concerns.

#### 5.2 Day 60 Milestone: First Project Delivered

**Objective:** The new hire has independently completed a meaningful work deliverable and is contributing actively to team goals.

**Expected outcomes by Day 60:**
- Delivered at least **1 project or significant work item** with guidance from their manager or buddy.
- Participated in at least 2 cross-functional meetings or workshops.
- Demonstrated proficiency with core tools and systems (Jira workflow completion rate above 90%).
- Received initial peer feedback (minimum 2 colleagues) via the BambooHR feedback module.
- Identified at least 1 area for professional development and discussed it with their manager.
- Manager conducts a **60-day check-in** to evaluate contribution quality and cultural fit.

#### 5.3 Day 90 Milestone: Performance Checkpoint

**Objective:** The new hire is performing at the expected level for their role and is fully integrated into the team.

**Expected outcomes by Day 90:**
- Completed the **90-Day Performance Checkpoint** form (self-assessment + manager assessment).
- Achieved at least **"Meets Expectations"** (rating of 3 or above) in the checkpoint review.
- Established a **6-month development plan** with the manager, including at least 2 SMART goals.
- Probation decision made: confirm employment, extend probation (by 30 days max), or terminate.
- Standard probation period is **60 days** per Vietnamese Labor Law; GravityTech uses the Day 90 checkpoint as a comprehensive review point.

**Failure to meet Day 90 expectations** may result in:
- Probation extension (maximum 30 additional days) with a documented improvement plan.
- In rare cases, termination with notice as per the employment contract.

All training completion records are maintained in BambooHR and reviewed during annual compliance audits.

---

### 7. Onboarding Buddy Program

- Buddies are assigned by the hiring manager before the new hire's start date.
- The buddy commitment is **90 days**, with an expected time investment of **2-3 hours per week**.
- Buddies should schedule informal check-ins at least twice per week during the first month, then weekly for months 2-3.
- Buddies receive a **500,000 VND meal voucher** as recognition for each successful onboarding completed.
