## 1. Scope and Purpose

This document outlines the standard terms and conditions applicable to all vendor contracts entered into by Gravity Tech Solutions Joint Stock Company ("Company"), Tax Code 0123456789, with its registered office at Floor 12, Landmark 81, 720A Dien Bien Phu, Ward 22, Binh Thanh District, Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam.

These terms apply unless explicitly superseded by a separately negotiated Master Services Agreement (MSA).

## 2. Payment Terms

### 2.1 Payment Schedule

| Term | Detail |
|---|---|
| Payment terms | **Net 30** (payment due within 30 calendar days of invoice receipt) |
| Payment method | **Bank transfer only** |
| Bank | **Joint Stock Commercial Bank for Foreign Trade of Vietnam (Vietcombank)** |
| Currency | Vietnamese Dong (VND), unless otherwise agreed in writing |
| Invoice submission | Electronic invoice (e-invoice) required per Decree 123/2020/ND-CP |

### 2.2 Payment Conditions

- Invoices must reference the **Purchase Order (PO) number**.
- Payment is contingent on satisfactory delivery and acceptance of deliverables.
- Early payment discounts: **2% discount for payment within 10 days** (Net 10/2%).
- Late payment interest: **0.03% per day** of overdue amount (per Article 306, Commercial Law 2005).

### 2.3 Withholding Tax

- The Company will withhold **Foreign Contractor Tax (FCT)** as required by Vietnamese tax law for foreign vendors:
  - Corporate Income Tax (CIT): 5% for services, 10% for royalties.
  - Value Added Tax (VAT): 5% for services.
- Vietnamese vendors are responsible for their own tax obligations.

## 3. Warranty

### 3.1 Warranty Periods

| Deliverable Type | Warranty Period |
|---|---|
| Software (custom development) | **12 months** from final acceptance |
| Software (licenses / SaaS) | Duration of license/subscription term |
| Hardware | **24 months** from delivery and installation |
| Professional services | **6 months** from project completion |

### 3.2 Warranty Scope

During the warranty period, the Vendor shall:

- Correct any defects, errors, or non-conformities at **no additional cost**.
- Provide bug fixes and patches within the following timeframes:
  - Critical defects (system down): **4 hours** response, **24 hours** resolution.
  - Major defects (significant impact): **8 hours** response, **3 business days** resolution.
  - Minor defects (limited impact): **1 business day** response, **10 business days** resolution.
- Maintain backward compatibility for any patches or updates.

### 4.1 Availability

## 5. Intellectual Property Ownership

**5.1** All deliverables created under the contract, including but not limited to source code, documentation, designs, data models, and configurations, shall be the **exclusive property of the Company** upon full payment.

**5.2** The Vendor assigns all rights, title, and interest in the deliverables to the Company, including all intellectual property rights (copyright, patent, trade secret).

**5.3** The Vendor retains rights to its pre-existing intellectual property ("Background IP"), which shall be licensed to the Company on a **perpetual, irrevocable, royalty-free, non-exclusive basis** to the extent incorporated into the deliverables.

**5.4** The Vendor warrants that deliverables do not infringe any third-party intellectual property rights and shall indemnify the Company against any infringement claims.

**5.5** All source code must be delivered with complete build instructions and shall be held in escrow via **Iron Mountain** escrow services.

## 6. Liability

### 6.1 Liability Cap

The Vendor's total aggregate liability under the contract shall not exceed **200% of the total contract value**.

### 6.2 Exclusions from Cap

The liability cap does not apply to:

- Intellectual property infringement claims.
- Breaches of confidentiality obligations.
- Willful misconduct or gross negligence.
- Data breaches involving the Company's data.
- Indemnification obligations.

### 6.3 Limitation of Liability

Neither Party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, or punitive damages, except in cases of willful misconduct, gross negligence, or breach of confidentiality.

## 7. Termination

### 7.1 Termination for Convenience

Either Party may terminate the contract by providing **thirty (30) days written notice** to the other Party.

Upon termination for convenience:
- The Company shall pay for all deliverables accepted up to the termination date.
- The Vendor shall provide a pro-rata refund for any prepaid services not yet rendered.

### 7.2 Termination for Cause (Material Breach)

Either Party may terminate the contract **immediately upon written notice** if the other Party:

- Commits a material breach and fails to cure within **fifteen (15) days** of written notice.
- Becomes insolvent, bankrupt, or enters liquidation proceedings.
- Is found to have engaged in fraud, corruption, or illegal activity.
- Fails to meet SLA requirements for **three (3) consecutive months**.

### 7.3 Transition Assistance

Upon termination (for any reason), the Vendor shall:

- Provide transition assistance for up to **90 days** at standard rates.
- Transfer all Company data in machine-readable format within **30 days**.
- Continue providing services during the transition period if requested.
- Delete all Company data from Vendor systems within **60 days** of transition completion, with written certification.

## 8. Insurance

The Vendor must maintain the following insurance coverage throughout the contract term:

| Insurance Type | Minimum Coverage |
|---|---|
| Professional indemnity / Errors & Omissions | **10,000,000,000 VND** (10 billion VND) |
| General commercial liability | **5,000,000,000 VND** (5 billion VND) |
| Cyber liability (for IT vendors) | **5,000,000,000 VND** (5 billion VND) |
| Workers' compensation | As required by Vietnamese law |

- Insurance certificates must be provided **before contract execution** and renewed annually.
- The Company must be named as an **additional insured** on the professional indemnity policy.
- The Vendor must notify the Company within **10 business days** of any material change in coverage.

## 9. Confidentiality

**9.1** The Vendor shall treat all Company information as confidential and comply with the Company's standard NDA (Template LEGAL-NDA-2025-v2.3).

**9.2** The Vendor shall not use the Company's name, logo, or trademarks in marketing materials without prior written consent.

## 10. Data Protection

**10.1** The Vendor shall comply with all applicable data protection laws, including Vietnam's Personal Data Protection Decree (Decree 13/2023/ND-CP).

**10.2** The Vendor shall execute a **Data Processing Agreement (DPA)** if processing personal data on behalf of the Company.

**10.3** Cross-border data transfers require explicit Company approval and must comply with applicable transfer mechanisms.

## 11. Compliance and Ethics

**11.1** The Vendor shall comply with all applicable Vietnamese laws and regulations.

**11.2** The Vendor represents and warrants that it shall not engage in any form of bribery, corruption, or fraud.

**11.3** The Vendor shall comply with the Company's Anti-Corruption Policy, including the gift policy (maximum value: 2,000,000 VND per gift).

## 12. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

**12.1** This contract is governed by the **laws of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam**.

**12.2** Disputes shall be resolved through good-faith negotiation within **30 days**, failing which the dispute shall be submitted to the **Vietnam International Arbitration Centre (VIAC)**.
